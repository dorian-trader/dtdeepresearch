✅ Why these qualify for PPP/SIRRIPA modeling
High growth / delayed profitability: Firms like Amazon, Tesla, and Snowflake operate with thin or negative margins for extended periods. Traditional P/E heavily undervalues such profiles, while PPP (as shown by Sam 2025) effectively handles these edge cases 
rainsysam.com

Option-like earnings: As explicitly tested alongside NVIDIA and Broadcom in Sam’s case studies 
rainsysam.com
, PPP generalizes well to any firm where future earnings drive present value disproportionately.

Earnings growth misalignment: Companies such as Salesforce, Netflix, Snowflake, and PayPal still reinvest heavily, suppressing current earnings—perfect for a PPP framework over P/E or PEG 
rainsysam.com

The models aren’t limited to tech—any firm with delayed payback horizons (e.g., bio/pharma Moderna) or capital-intensive long-turnaround (e.g., Tesla) fits.
